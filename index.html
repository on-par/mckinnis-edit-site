The edit

Every image is a choice. A point of view. An edit.

The McKinnis Edit sits between fine art photography and commercial brand work. Not a budget studio, not an agency queue. A working partnership with one photographer who learns your business before he picks up a camera.

The result is a set of images that hold together. They look like you on your clearest day, and they keep working long after the session ends.

About

Michael McKinnis

Michael has spent nearly thirty years making photographs. The studio was formerly known as Eye Soul Photography and was reestablished as The McKinnis Edit to reflect a more intentional, editorial approach to visual storytelling. The name is the promise: refinement, curation, a point of view.

He works with entrepreneurs, business owners and professionals across Texas, including San Antonio, Austin, Houston and Dallas. Clients tend to arrive at a threshold. A launch, a new role, a milestone. That is the work he likes best.

Cerissa McKinnis

Michael's wife, Cerissa, is a key creative and brand advisor to the studio. She is the author of Navigating the Liminal Places, a book about life's transitional moments seen through the lens of interior design. That sensibility, the belief that a space and a season shape how a person shows up, runs straight through the way this studio sees a room and the person standing in it.
